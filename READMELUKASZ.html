DopeNasty Stealth Game

A game by Nick Kosichek and Scott Kaye.

Controls

Keystroke Effect
WASD Movement
B Toggle Hitboxes
G Toggle GodMode
ESC Quit

Objective

The goal of the game is to reach the Stairs entity without being caught. As you travel through the level, enemies draw sight-lines to you and if there is nothing blocking the sight-line, their awareness meter grows. If any enemy has a full awareness meter, you are caught and the game is over. If you make it through all the stages without being caught, you win!

Tips

  • Don’t get caught!
  • If you’re having trouble, toggle godmode.
